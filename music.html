Music Site

Professional Presentation

Date: Spring 2013

Explanation: For my English class, we had to create a website for a music artist's song. In the website, we were to talk about the artist, the songs literary elements, and the songs meaning. We were to present the site to the class. I chose to do Madonna's Like a Prayer. It just really is a feel good song with a great, uplifting meaning.

Reflection:I decided to do something different with my site. This project allowed me freedom to experiment and to try new things. One of the first things I did was to have a navigation on the bottom to coalesce with the background image.
